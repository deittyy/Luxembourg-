LUXEMBOURG

Luxembourg is the richest country in the world per capital GDP. It is a small landlocked country located in Western Europe, bordered by Belgium, France, and Germany. Despite its size, it's a significant financial and economic hub,with a high standard of living, low unemployment and a strong economy. It is also known for multinational corporations due to its favourable tax environment.

According to Jean Claude Juncker, former Prime minister of Luxembourg:

Luxembourg is a small country, but it has a huge heart and a strong will.
  • Capital: Luxembourg City
  • Language: Luxembourgish, French, German and English.
  • Population: Approximately 590,000
  • Currency: Euro
